Fastest Delivery

Delivery will be between 8am and 7pm. You will receive an ETA on the morning of delivery. You do not have to be in to receive your box. Your box will be left in the safe designated space. It will be kept fresh with the ice packs and insulated cooler bags/boxes We aim to dispatch all meals within 24hours of your order coming in, and for you to receive within 48hrs of placing your orders. An exception to this would be when meals aren’t readily available, a special request has been put in, or orders placed on Fridays and the weekend There is no minimum order for delivery. Free delivery is offered on orders over £99*

Original Recipes

Our meals are dairy-free, no sugar, preservatives or additives. We offer a range of dishes from poultry only, to fish only, to no-fish dishes and so on. We’re at the moment unable to offer a bespoke menu. However, as our menu is ever-expanding, you may let us know what you would like and we will strongly consider adding it to our menu.
